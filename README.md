## ğŸ™‹ FAQs

**Q: How accurate is DeepAuth?**  
A: DeepAuth cross-references multiple credible sources. However, it's AI-powered and should be one tool in your fact-checking toolkit. Always check sources yourself for critical decisions.

**Q: What sources does it use?**  
A: DeepAuth searches reputable news outlets, official government sites, academic sources, and verified social media accounts. All sources are shown with results.

**Q: Can I trust it for election decisions?**  
A: DeepAuth helps you verify information, but always cross-check important claims yourself. We encourage critical thinking.

**Q: Is my data stored?**  
A: No. We don't store your searches or personal information.

**Q: Can I use it offline?**  
A: Not yet, but offline mode is on our roadmap for areas with limited connectivity.

**Q: How can I report an error?**  
A: Click "Report Issue" on any fact-check result or [open a GitHub issue](https://github.com/adriel-babalola/deep_auth/issues).
